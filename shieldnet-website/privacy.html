Privacy Policy - ShieldNet AI

Last Updated: 29/12/2025

ShieldNet AI is operated by SHIELDNETCORE TECHNOLOGIES. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to provide safety-related features:

  • Location data (foreground and background, with user permission)
  • Device information
  • App usage data
  • Audio metadata during SOS activation (only when explicitly triggered)

We do not sell personal data.

How We Use Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide real-time safety alerts
  • Enable SOS emergency features
  • Improve threat awareness accuracy
  • Maintain app performance and reliability

Data Sharing

We may share limited information only with:

  • Emergency contacts you select
  • Emergency responders during SOS activation
  • Service providers supporting app infrastructure

Data Security

We use encryption, access controls, and secure cloud services to protect user data.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time
  • You can delete your account and associated data
  • You control SOS and contact permissions

Children's Privacy

ShieldNet AI is not intended for children under 13.
